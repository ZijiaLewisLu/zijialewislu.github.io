About Me

I am a Member of Technical Staff at Physion Labs, working on world critic model. Prior to this, I was a Senior Research Scientist at Tencent Hunyuan, working on Full-Duplex Dialogue LLM.

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science at Northeastern University. My research focuses on Video Understanding Methods to address key challenges in Video-Language Models (VLM) and Assistant AI. During my Ph.D. study, I have had the opportunity to spend two memorable internships at Amazon and Microsoft. In the past, I also worked as a Student Researcher at Chinese Academic of Sciences.
